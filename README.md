## 🎯 What I Do

I'm an AI/ML engineer who turns ideas into working, deployed products. I'm strongest in the
**voice and speech domain**, where I build and refine recognition systems and bring them all the
way to production. I move comfortably across the whole stack: training and evaluating models,
wrapping them in clean APIs, and shipping polished interfaces on top. I learn fast, ship
consistently, and care about building things people actually use.
